sous-vide shredded beef chuck

Description:

A great and easy way to turn a cheap cut of meat into one of the most delicious pieces of food in existence.

  • beef chuck roast
  • seasoning to taste
  1. Preheat sous vide circulator to 175F
  2. Cut your beef into 2 inch chunks and sear on all sides on high heat
  3. Bag your meat and use the water displacement method to remove the majority of the air from the container
  4. Cook for 24 hours
  5. Remove the meat from the bag and refrigerate or use straight away, save the juices from the bag and store in the fridge and use the layer of fat on top for gravy or frying vegetables, and save the juice for usage as stock or in a sauce or something else
